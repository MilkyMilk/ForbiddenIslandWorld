User Guide:
Your mission should you choose to accept it, is to become the biggest fish in the sea.  
Use the arrow keys to avoid fish bigger than yourself.  To grow, collide with a smaller fish.  
You will lose the game if you are eaten by a larger fish.  You will win if you become the biggest 
fish in the sea.  To quit the game, hit X.  Good luck big tuna.
